Frequenty Asked Questions

You can log in to the system by clicking the log in button on the navigation bar positioned at the top of the website. The page will then lead you to the log in page which will ask for your log in credentials.

If you are a resident of barangay Malagutay you cannot own an account in this system because the website is meant for the officials and staff of barangay Malagutay.

If you are a staff or official of barangay Malagutay you can ask the admin or other account holders to register your account on the system.

On the log in page there will be a link that says forgot password below the password input field. You can click this and there will be instructions that tell you how to reset your password. Follow the instructions properly and you should be able to reset your password and log back in.

The system specializes in storing and processing data of residents of barangay Malagutay. The system also comes with a data visualization feature to make it easier to gain insights on the data gathered. Additionally, the system can also generate documents such as certificates and reports.

If you are a resident and you want to send your information to the system you must first verify to the barangay officials and staff if you are really not registered to the system.

Your information will be sent to the system in two ways:

  • First is that the barangay officials or staff can input your information on their end without you having to do anything.
  • Second is that you can turn in a registration by clicking on the registration button on the navigation menu. You will then be sent to a form page where you need to input the information required. After submission, your form will undergo a validation process where the officials or staff can either accept or decline your registration. An email notification will be sent to your inputted email after validation.

There are two ways you can get a certification on the system:

  • First is that you can contact the barangay officials or staff and tell them that you need a certification. They will take care of everything on their end and you can receive you certification when they inform you.
  • Second is that you can submit a certification request by clicking the certification button on the navigation menu. It will lead you to a page with a form that you need to fill out. You will need your resident code in order to request a certificate. You contact the barangay officials/staff for your code. If you registered by submitting a form on this system then you can refer to the email that was sent to you.

If an error pops up and says that you are not eligible for a certification, it is possible that you have a blotter report case and are blacklisted from receiving any form of certificate. You can contact the barangay officials or staff to resolve this issue. When the issue is resolved they will take care of things on their end and you should be able to request a certification.
