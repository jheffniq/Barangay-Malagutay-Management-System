About
Welcome to Barangay Malagutay Management System!

Barangay Malagutay Management System is a web-based application that aims to help barangay officials manage and monitor the barangay comfortably and conveniently.

This web-application is accessible to all the staff and barangay officials of barangay Malagutay. Account holders may log in to the system using their credentials.

About Barangay Malagutay

Malagutay is a barangay in Zamboanga City. Its population represents 0.85% of the total population of Zamboanga City. Malagutay is situated approximately 6.9390, 122.0163, in the island of Mindanao and borders the barangays of San Jose Gusu, Capisan, San Roque, Maasin, Sinunoc, and Calarian.
